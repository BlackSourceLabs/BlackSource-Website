Our Why

More important than what we do, how long we have been working, or what titles we hold, is why. By starting off the conversation with why, you can get a clear understanding of our intentions and our purpose.

So why do we do this?

Action is the best religion.
When you ask people why they start businesses, most of them tell you that they want to go into business for themselves. To them we say that without customers to serve, there is no business. We believe inextricably in going to business for others, not ourselves.

We do this because someone is out in the rain homeless, and without a drop of hope. We do this for that young lad who stays in the after-school program because it provides him an extra meal for the day. We do this because the wonderful ecologies that sustain life are under threat.

Our Purpose

What keeps us going?

Our purpose is to enhance the lives of the underserved by using Technology.

We believe in what we call the bottom-up approach. By creating solutions that benefit the lowest rungs of society, we help all members of society advance.
